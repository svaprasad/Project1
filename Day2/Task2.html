About me

Tigers are large, powerful, orange and black striped wild cats and the national animal of India. They are carnivorous predators, hunting with their sharp teeth, claws, and silent, padded paws to ambush prey in forests. As the largest cats, tigers are vital to their ecosystem but are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching, making their conservation crucial.
